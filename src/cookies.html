Cookie Policy

Welcome to Infera-Block! To improve your experience with our website, ensure its proper functioning, and analyze user activity, we use cookies and similar technologies. This policy explains in detail what technologies we use, for what purposes, and how you can manage them.

What are cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of data that a website stores on your device when you visit it. This allows the platform to "remember" your actions and preferences over a period of time.

What types of cookies do we use?

We classify cookies on the Infera-Block website into the following categories:

  • Strictly Necessary: Essential for the website to function and provide access to secure sections.
  • Analytical: Help us understand how you interact with the site (e.g., Google Analytics). All data is anonymous.
  • Functional: Remember your choices (language, region) for a more personalized experience.
  • Marketing: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of campaigns.

Why do we use cookies?

Our main objectives at Infera-Block are:

  • Ensuring stable and secure platform operation.
  • Analyzing user behavior to optimize content structure.
  • Personalizing career recommendations.
  • Providing marketing materials in the UK and EU countries.

Your choice and management

Most modern browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through settings. However, please note: disabling necessary cookies may restrict access to certain features of Infera-Block.
