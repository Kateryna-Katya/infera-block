Personal Data Processing Policy

1. General Provisions

This Personal Data Processing Policy (hereinafter — the "Policy") defines the procedure and conditions for processing personal data undertaken by the Infera-Block platform (hereinafter — the "Operator") and establishes measures to ensure the security of this data in accordance with UK GDPR requirements.

1.1. The Operator considers the observance of human and civil rights and freedoms when processing personal data, including the protection of the right to privacy, personal and family confidentiality, to be its most important goal and condition for its activities.

1.2. This Policy applies to all information that the Operator may obtain about visitors (hereinafter — "Users") of the website infera-block.blog.

2. Key Definitions Used in the Policy

  • Website — a collection of graphic and information materials, as well as computer programs available on the Internet at infera-block.blog.
  • User — any visitor to the website.
  • Personal Data — any information relating directly or indirectly to a specific User of the website.
  • Processing of Personal Data — any action performed with personal data, including collection, recording, storage, clarification, use, and destruction of data.

3. Data We Process

The Operator may process the following categories of data:

  • Personal data provided by the User:
    • Full name;
    • Email address;
    • Phone number (digits only).
  • Automatically collected data:
    • The site collects anonymized data about visitors ("cookies") using Google Analytics and other analysis tools to improve the resource's performance.

4. Purposes of Personal Data Processing

  • Identification of the User to provide career consultations.
  • Establishing feedback, including sending notifications about new blog materials.
  • Execution of agreements and requests submitted via the form on the website.
  • Informing the User via email newsletters. You can always opt-out of notifications by writing to hello@infera-block.blog with the subject "Unsubscribe".

5. Legal Grounds

The Operator processes personal data only with the voluntary consent of the User, expressed through filling out feedback forms on the website.

6. Data Collection and Security Procedures

  • The Operator takes technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access.
  • Personal data is never transferred to third parties, except in cases provided for by UK legislation.
  • The processing period is unlimited or until the User withdraws their consent.

7. Final Provisions

  • For clarifications on the processing of your data, please contact us by email: hello@infera-block.blog.
