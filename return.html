Return Policy

Refund Eligibility Conditions

You may be eligible for a full or partial refund for Infera-Block consulting services or digital programs in the following situations:

  • Service Inconsistency: If the content of the provided consultation or program significantly differs from the plan stated on our website at the time of purchase.
  • Technical Malfunctions: In case of critical issues on the Infera-Block platform that make it impossible to access paid materials and were not resolved by our support team within 3 working days.
  • Cooling-off Period Withdrawal: In accordance with UK legislation, you have the right to cancel a service within 14 calendar days of payment, provided you have not yet accessed digital materials or the service has not yet been rendered.

Refund Request Procedure

To initiate a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Compose an email and send it to our official address: hello@infera-block.blog.
  2. In the subject line, specify: "Refund Request".
  3. Include in the email: your full name, the email address used for the purchase, and the name of the selected service/program.
  4. Provide a detailed description of the reason for the request, referring to the refund eligibility conditions.
  5. Our support team will review the application within 48 hours and contact you for confirmation.
